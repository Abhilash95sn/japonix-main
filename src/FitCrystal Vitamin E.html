    FitCrystal Vitamin E – A Pure, Stable, and Highly Bioavailable Form of Vitamin E

    FitCrystal Vitamin E is a crystalline powder produced through the co-crystallization of natural Vitamin E (α-tocopherol) with purified plant oil extracts. This innovative formulation retains the natural molecular structure while offering exceptional chemical stability, superior flowability, and enhanced processability. Its crystalline form makes it ideal for various solid dosage formulations, including tablets, capsules, and powders.

    What is Natural Vitamin E?

    Vitamin E is a fat-soluble antioxidant that exists in two forms:

  • Natural Vitamin E (d-α-tocopherol) – The biologically active form found in plant oils.
  • Synthetic Vitamin E (a mix of d-α-tocopherol + 7 other isomers) – A lab-made version with lower bioactivity.

    • Among these, natural d-α-tocopherol is the most potent and effective form, delivering the primary biological benefits of Vitamin E.

    Key Health Benefits of Natural Vitamin E

    • ✔ Supports Reproductive Health – Improves fertility and helps prevent miscarriage.
    • ✔ Enhances Heart Health – Promotes lipid metabolism and reduces the risk of cardiovascular diseases.
    • ✔ Boosts Immunity – Strengthens immune function and lowers the incidence of respiratory diseases.
    • ✔ Delays Aging – Protects cells from oxidative stress, slowing down the aging process.

    Why Choose FitCrystal Vitamin E?

    Challenges with Conventional Vitamin E

    Natural Vitamin E is typically a viscous oil that is highly unstable when exposed to light and heat, making it unsuitable for direct use in solid dosage forms.

    To address this, the industry commonly relies on:

    • Chemical derivatization – Alters the natural properties of Vitamin E.
    • Microencapsulation – Reduces antioxidant activity and bioavailability.

    The FitCrystal Vitamin E Advantage

    FitCrystal Vitamin E is the only active powdered form of natural d-α-tocopherol that converts the oily Vitamin E into a highly flowable crystalline powder—without chemical modification or microencapsulation. This ensures pure, stable, and highly bioavailable Vitamin E.

    • Superior Bioavailability – Optimized for maximum absorption and utilization.
    • Enhanced Stability – Resistant to degradation from heat and light.
    • Excellent Antioxidant Activity – Effective in both in vivo and in vitro studies.
    • High Purity & Content – Contains up to 80% natural Vitamin E.
